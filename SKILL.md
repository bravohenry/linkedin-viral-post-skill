# LinkedIn Viral Post Creator

Transform ideas into high-engagement LinkedIn content using data-driven patterns from 40+ posts with 100,000+ impressions.

## Overview

This skill applies battle-tested principles for creating LinkedIn posts that:
- Hook readers in the preview window
- Balance professionalism with humanity
- Share authentic insights from your domain
- Capitalize on trending moments (when relevant)

**Core Philosophy**: LinkedIn favors genuine expertise shared generously, not corporate speak or humble brags.

## Workflow

### Step 1: Craft the Hook (First Two Sentences)

**Why it matters**: LinkedIn preview shows only first 2 lines. These determine click-through.

#### Pattern A: Outcome-First Hook

```markdown
I dropped out from Berkeley and now I'm a billionaire

Here's how I did it without a degree:
```

**Template**:
```
[Past state] ‚Üí [Current impressive state]

[Promise to reveal the process]:
```

#### Pattern B: Numbers-First Hook

```markdown
I have 40+ LinkedIn posts with 100,000+ impressions

Here's 3 simple steps to instantly make viral posts:
```

**Template**:
```
[Specific achievement with numbers]

Here's [X actionable steps] to [desired outcome]:
```

#### Pattern C: Contrarian Take

```markdown
Everyone's posting AI-generated content. They're tanking their engagement.

I manually write every post. Here's why it works:
```

**Template**:
```
[Common behavior] is wrong. [Consequence].

[Your different approach]. Here's why:
```

**Rules**:
- Lead with concrete outcome or numbers
- Create curiosity gap between state A and state B
- Promise a process, not just a result
- Keep under 30 words total

Track these to improve:

| Metric | Good | Great | Viral |

1. Post ‚Üí track metrics in 48 hours

## Rules

1. **Hook is everything**: First 2 sentences determine success
2. **Stay in your lane**: Share your domain expertise
3. **Numbers build credibility**: Specific metrics beat vague claims
4. **Group photos win**: 2-3 people, professional context
5. **Format for skimmers**: Short paragraphs, clear structure
6. **Speed beats perfection**: For trends, post within 24 hours
7. **Engage immediately**: Respond to comments in first hour
8. **Be genuinely helpful**: Share insights, not self-promotion

## When NOT to Use This Skill

- Personal/private updates (job changes, life events) - be authentic, not formulaic
- Commenting on others' posts - be conversational
- Company announcements - follow brand guidelines
- Controversial topics - tread carefully, this is a professional platform
- You have nothing new to say - don't post for the sake of posting
